What’s all this, then? 

This is a plugin meant to display your screenplay divided into one column per act, or whatever else you want to call your topmost-level, single-hashtag section type.

It’s most useful if you’re the kind of writer who likes to divide your scripts into #ACTS, ##SEQUENCES, and perhaps ###SUB-SEQUENCES (and so on) and will give you a real-time bird’s eye-view of your story.
 







You can also open (and dismiss) this dialog by hitting I for ”info”.  
 
NOTE: Page lengths are approximations, calculated from the number of lines in each scene. Since Beat will often prefer to move an entire element to the next page rather than split them, the actual page count/page number may well differ by a page or two compared to the number here.
